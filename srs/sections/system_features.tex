\section{System Features}

The “11th Lesson” e-learning platform helps both the students and teachers with fast learning and enlarging the area of academic tutorials.

Dashboard: Separate dashboards for student and teacher with an organized list of course materials.

Publish Feature: A feature that allows users to upload their own notes and classes on the platform both publicly and privately.

Customizability: Users can customize their own dashboard according to their learning abilities.

Ratings: Learners can rate the uploaded learning materials to vote their preferences.

Assignment Submission and Grading: Enable teachers to create and assign homework or projects to students. Students can submit their work directly through the platform, and teachers can review, grade, and provide feedback.

Progress Tracking and Analytics: Provide a progress tracking system where students can see their performance, complete courses, and learning milestones. Teachers can view analytics on student engagement, completion rates, and performance insights.

Offline Reading: This feature allows student to download content and read it later.

Demo Quiz: Students can check their learnings through online demo quizzes.

Collaborative Learning Spaces: Allow students to create or join study groups, share resources, and discuss topics in a collaborative environment.

Live Sessions: Offer real-time live classes where teachers can interact with students, answer questions, and provide instant feedback.
